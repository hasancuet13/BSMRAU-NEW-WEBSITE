vice chancellor

Welcome to Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Agricultural University (BSMRAU) which is dedicated to its vision of providing quality education and research to produce graduates capable of handling both local and global emerging challenges in agriculture. The university has been glorified for being the first quality education dispenser in the country, which is named after the name of the Father of the Nation of Bangladesh. BSMRAU was established in 1998 by upgrading the Institute of Post-graduate Studies in Agriculture (IPSA), which was set up in 1991 as an autonomous institution by upgrading Bangladesh College of Agricultural Science (BCAS). The university is located 35 km north of Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. Its serene campus with beauty and bounty of nature attracts everybody to come to the campus time and again. Its academic excellence mainly relies on its efficient academia and course-credit based trimester system of education that strictly follows academic calendar which of course has guaranteed timely completion of academic programs. The university maintains strong linkage with various higher educational institutions, universities, and research organizations at regional and global levels to conduct globally competitive research using frontier technologies, especially in agriculture....
